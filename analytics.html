What we deliver

An analytics operating system, not a slide deck.

We connect your media, product and commercial data, then turn it into the insights that change outcomes.

Measurement frameworks

KPI trees, event taxonomies and governance, mapped to revenue, margin, LTV and CAC. Documented and audit-ready from day one.

Tracking & implementation

GA4, Google Tag Manager (web and server-side), Firebase for mobile and Adobe Analytics. Full audits, rebuilds and clean handover documentation.

Conversion recovery & attribution

Meta CAPI, Google Enhanced Conversions, TikTok Events API and modeled attribution. Recover lost conversions and defend budget with evidence, not guesswork.

Ecommerce & funnel tracking

Enhanced ecommerce for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento and custom platforms. Lead-gen and SaaS funnels with drop-off and revenue attribution by source.

Data engineering & warehouse

BigQuery or Snowflake, modeled with dbt, fed by ELT pipelines. Segment or Tealium when activation matters. Single source of truth, owned by you.

Dashboards & data science

Looker Studio, Power BI and Tableau dashboards built around the questions they answer. Segmentation, propensity, churn and forecasting where it earns its keep.

Our stack

Tooling we run every day

A modern, best-in-class analytics stack, owned end-to-end by the Adventis data team.

  • Tracking: GA4, GTM (web & server-side), Firebase, Adobe Analytics.
  • Conversion APIs: Meta CAPI, Google Enhanced Conversions, TikTok Events API.
  • Consent & privacy: Consent Mode v2, OneTrust, Cookiebot, Didomi.
  • CDP: Segment, Tealium, RudderStack.
  • Warehouse: BigQuery, Snowflake, SQL, dbt.
  • Ecommerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, custom APIs.
  • Modeling: Python, R, Jupyter, Vertex AI.
  • Visualisation: Looker Studio, Power BI, Tableau.
  • Integrations: Google Ads, Meta, TikTok, DV360, CRM, payments, support.

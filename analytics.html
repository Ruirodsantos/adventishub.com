What we deliver

An analytics operating system, not a slide deck.

We connect your media, product and commercial data, then deliver the insights that change outcomes.

We map KPI trees to business outcomes like revenue, margin, LTV and CAC. Event and conversion taxonomies with GA4 + GTM implementation and audits.

Funnel analytics across acquisition, activation and retention. Creative intelligence and attribution models aligned to reality.

Warehouses in BigQuery or Snowflake, ELT pipelines and connectors for Ads, CRM and payments. Daily QA and anomaly detection keep trust high.

Executive and squad dashboards in Looker Studio or Power BI, with automated weekly summaries. Insights, not screenshots.

Segmentation, propensity, churn and demand forecasting guide budget and lifecycle flows. When it pays off, we lean on Vertex AI.

Privacy by design

Measurement you can trust, data you still own.

Consent-aware tracking, data minimisation and strict access governance, with your data staying in your warehouse and under your ownership. When cookies are limited, we rely on modeled conversions, first-party data and clean-room workflows to keep measurement compliant and credible.

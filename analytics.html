What we deliver

An analytics operating system, not a slide deck.

We connect your media, product and commercial data, then deliver the insights that change outcomes.

We start with the business, not the tag. KPI trees map directly to revenue, margin, LTV and CAC, with clear definitions and ownership for every metric. Event taxonomies, conversion definitions and data governance are documented so your tracking scales as the business grows.

What you get: measurement plan, KPI dictionary, event spec, governance model and audit-ready documentation.

Full implementation across web and app: GA4 with custom events and parameters, Google Tag Manager (with version control and naming conventions), Firebase for native iOS and Android, plus cross-platform user-journey stitching. We audit and rebuild legacy setups so the data your team relies on is actually trustworthy.

What you get: clean GA4 + GTM (web & mobile), enhanced ecommerce, custom events, debug suites and handover docs.

A Google Tag Manager Server-Side container moves tracking off the browser and onto your own infrastructure. That means iOS 14+ and ad-blocker losses get recovered, page performance improves, and you keep tight control over what data leaves your site. We design the container, route tags through it and harden security and consent end-to-end.

Typical impact: 30–50% more event data captured versus client-side only, faster pages, lower data leakage risk.

Meta CAPI, Google Enhanced Conversions, TikTok Events API and equivalents for every major platform. We deduplicate against pixel data, send rich first-party signals to ad platforms and rebuild attribution where cookies fail. On top of that, we run multi-touch and modeled attribution so you can defend budget allocation with evidence, not guesswork.

Typical impact: 20–40% conversions recovered for Meta-led brands, sharper ROAS, fewer "missing" outcomes in reporting.

Enhanced ecommerce for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento and custom platforms, plus dedicated lead-gen and SaaS funnels. We track the full journey, from first ad impression to product view, cart, checkout, repeat purchase and lifecycle, then expose drop-off points so growth, product and creative teams can act on them.

What you get: end-to-end funnel visibility, cart-abandonment signals, lead-quality scoring and revenue attribution by source.

Warehouses in BigQuery or Snowflake, modeled with dbt and connected to Ads, CRM, payments, support and product data via ELT pipelines. Customer Data Platforms (Segment, Tealium) sit in front when activation matters. Daily QA, anomaly detection and SLAs keep trust high — and keep your data inside your own warehouse, under your own ownership.

What you get: a single source of truth, automated pipelines, modeled tables and monitoring you can audit.

Executive overviews, squad-level dashboards and automated weekly summaries in Looker Studio, Power BI or Tableau. We focus on decisions, not screenshots: every dashboard is built around the question it answers, with thresholds, narratives and drill-throughs that turn numbers into action. Training and documentation make sure your teams keep using them after launch.

Segmentation, propensity, churn and demand-forecasting models guide budget allocation and lifecycle flows. Marketing-mix and incrementality testing answer the bigger questions about what is really driving growth. When it pays off, we lean on Vertex AI and modern ML tooling — but only when there's a measurable lift on the other side.

Celebrities & Influencers.

Partnerships with the voices your audience already trusts — celebrities, creators and influencers, matched to your brand and measured from the first post to the last conversion.

Overview

Credible voices, measurable outcomes.

People no longer buy products — they buy from people they believe in. We connect brands with celebrities and creators who actually fit your audience, and we run every partnership end-to-end: selection, negotiation, content direction, publishing, UTM tracking and reporting.

Whether you need a regional A-lister to launch a campaign, a tight roster of niche creators to drive performance, or a long-term ambassador programme, we design the right mix for your goals and budget — and we prove it worked.

What we do

Talent strategy, built for performance.

Regional and international celebrities — actors, musicians, athletes, public figures — integrated into your brand story through campaigns, appearances, endorsements and content series.

  • Talent identification & suitability checks (audience fit, brand safety, reputation)
  • Negotiation, contracts, exclusivity and usage rights
  • Creative direction, shoots and content approvals
  • Appearance & event management
  • PR amplification alongside paid media

Creator-led campaigns across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and X — built around real engagement, not vanity metrics. We shortlist on audience quality, not follower counts.

  • Verified creators across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and X
  • End-to-end outreach, contracts, content direction and approvals
  • Audience quality checks and brand safety validation
  • Integrated reporting with UTM tracking and performance metrics
  • Whitelisted ads and spark codes to scale what works

Long-term relationships that build consistent presence. Ambassadors become part of your brand story — not just a one-off post — with clear deliverables, KPIs and renewal paths.

  • Multi-quarter contracts with staged deliverables
  • Content calendars tied to product and seasonal beats
  • Always-on measurement and optimisation
  • Renewal, expansion and exit criteria defined upfront

Authentic, platform-native content produced by a roster of smaller creators — ideal as paid social ad fuel. High volume, fast turnaround, full usage rights.

  • Briefs, scripts and shot-lists tailored per platform
  • Roster of micro-creators across key verticals
  • Broad usage rights for paid media reuse
  • A/B testing framework to find winning hooks fast

For brands that want to build their own talent bench — or for creators looking for structured representation. We handle discovery, deals, content pipeline and commercial growth.

  • Talent scouting and onboarding
  • Brand deal sourcing and negotiation
  • Content production support and post-campaign analytics
  • Career planning, PR and crisis management
